I initiated a project aimed at addressing the inefficiencies in the IT asset management system at Kenbro Industries Limited. The existing system was outdated, leading to difficulties in tracking and managing IT assets. Statement of the Problem was; The primary problem was the lack of an efficient IT asset management system, resulting in:

  • Inaccurate tracking of IT assets.
  • Increased time spent on locating and maintaining assets.
  • Difficulty in planning for future IT needs and upgrades.
  • Seldom errors in information systems.

Project Methodology & Planning.
The project methodology involved the following steps:
  • Conducting an assessment of the current IT asset management process.
  • Identifying key areas of inefficiency and potential improvements.
  • Monitoring the system's performance and making necessary adjustments.

Project Execution
  • Assessment: Collected data on existing IT assets, including computers, software, and network equipment.
  • Design: Created a comprehensive plan for the new asset management sys-tem, incorporating features for tracking asset location, status, and mainte-nance history.
  • Implementation: Rolled out the new system in the IT department, provided training sessions for staff where necessary, and addressed initial challenges.
  • Monitoring: Continuously monitored the system's effectiveness, gathered feedback from users, and made
    • iterative improvements based on their in-put.

Project Outcome and Impact The new IT asset management system significantly improved the accuracy and efficiency. Key outcomes included:
  • Enhanced ability to quickly locate and manage IT assets.
  • Reduced time spent on asset maintenance and troubleshooting.
  • Improved planning for future IT needs and upgrades.
  • Increased overall efficiency of the IT department, allowing staff to focus on more critical tasks.
